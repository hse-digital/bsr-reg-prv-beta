Number of floors at or above ground level in {% if data['more-than-one-block'] === "Multiple independent sections" %} {{ data['block-name'] }} {% endif %} {% if data['more-than-one-block'] === "Single independent section" %} {{ data['building-name'] }} {% endif %}

Count all floors from ground level to the top floor that you walk on.

Include the following whether there's a residential unit or not:

  • any floors at or above ground level
  • any mezzanine floors that are at least 50% of the area of any of the other floors

Do not count:

  • floors below ground level
  • the roof
  • any mezzanine floors that are less than 50% of the area of any of the other floors
Help with ground level

Any ceiling below that level is below ground level.
